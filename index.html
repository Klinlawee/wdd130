Greater Accra, Ghana

Accra, the capital of Ghana, is a vibrant and bustling city located along the Gulf of Guinea. It serves as the country's economic, political, and cultural hub, blending modern infrastructure with rich historical landmarks. The city is home to key attractions like Independence Arch, Jamestown, and Makola Market, as well as beautiful beaches such as Labadi and Bojo. Accra boasts a lively arts and music scene, with venues like the National Theatre and the colorful Arts Centre. With its warm climate, diverse cuisine, and welcoming people, Accra offers a dynamic mix of tradition and modernity, making it a must-visit destination in West Africa.
